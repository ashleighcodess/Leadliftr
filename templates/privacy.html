Privacy Policy

Data We Don't Collect

LeadLiftr is designed with privacy by design principles:

  • No Personal Data Storage: We do not store any personal information from your CRM data
  • No User Accounts: No registration or personal information required
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • No Analytics: We do not collect usage statistics or behavioral data

How We Process Data

Temporary Processing Only:

  • Data is processed in real-time within your browser session
  • Server memory holds data only during active processing
  • All data is immediately purged after export completion
  • No logs containing personal data are maintained

Technical Safeguards

  • Memory-Only Processing: Data exists only in volatile memory
  • Automatic Cleanup: Data is automatically cleared after each session
  • No Database: No persistent storage of user data
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption

Your Control

You maintain complete control over your data:

  • You decide what data to upload and process
  • You control when data is exported and downloaded
  • You can close your session at any time to immediately purge all data
  • No data persists beyond your active session

Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or provide your data to any third parties. The service operates independently without external data sharing.

Server Logs

Standard web server logs may contain IP addresses and request information but do not include any CRM data content. These logs are used solely for technical maintenance and security purposes.

Privacy by Design: LeadLiftr is built to process your data without storing it. Your privacy is protected by the architecture of the application itself.
