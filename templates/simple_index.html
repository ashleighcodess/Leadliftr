Simple CRM Lead Extractor
No Chrome setup required!
How to use this tool:
  1. Go to your CRM page with the leads you want to export
  2. Right-click on the page and select "View Page Source" or press Ctrl+U
  3. Copy all the HTML code and paste it in the box below
  4. Choose a preset for your CRM or configure custom field mappings
  5. Click "Export to CSV" to download your leads
